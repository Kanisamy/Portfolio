UI/UX design

UI/UX design refers to the process of creating digital products that are both visually appealing (UI - User Interface) and easy to use (UX - User Experience). UI design focuses on the look and feel of a product, like buttons, layouts, and colors, while UX design ensures that the product is user-friendly, functional, and provides a smooth experience for users. Together, UI/UX design aims to make digital interactions intuitive and enjoyable.

App design

App design is the process of establishing an application's visual layout and user experience. It entails designing how the software appears (UI design) and how people interact with it (UX design). A good app design prioritises straightforward navigation, clear images, and seamless functionality across devices to ensure that the app is visually appealing, easy to use, and serves the demands of its users. It integrates aesthetics and usefulness to improve the entire experience.
